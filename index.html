Blog: Telecom Customer Churn Survival Analysis (Task 2)

1. Background & Purpose

Customer churn is critical for telecom companies. This analysis uses survival analysis to find key factors that affect customer retention and lifetime value.

2. Data & Workflow

Data: 3351 records, 1556 churn events.

Methods: Kaplan-Meier, Cox model, AFT model, Customer Final Value (CFV).

3. Key Findings

Top 5 significant features:

  • OnlineBackup
  • OnlineSecurity
  • Partner
  • PaymentMethod
  • TechSupport

Figure 1: Feature significance ranking table

OnlineBackup and OnlineSecurity are the most significant features.

Figure 2 & 3: Survival curves by OnlineBackup and OnlineSecurity

Customers with these services stay much longer.

Figure 4 & 5: Cox model results and hazard ratios

Value-added services reduce churn risk. Electronic check increases churn risk.

Figure 6 & 7: Log-log survival plots

Used to test model assumptions.

Figure 8 & 9: AFT model results

Value-added services extend customer lifetime.

Figure 10: 36-month survival probability

Good customers retain stably over time.

Figure 11 & 12: Customer Final Value (CFV)

High-value customers can reach over 900 yuan in 36 months.

4. Conclusion

  • OnlineBackup, OnlineSecurity, TechSupport greatly improve retention.
  • Check payments lead to higher churn.
  • Promote value-added services and better payment methods to reduce churn.
